What is this?

If you're like me, you're way behind in the Bachelor, but you can't catch up
because you don't remember who anybody is, and googling it would lead to
spoilers. I'm here to solve that problem. You can set the most recent episode
and will only see who has been eliminated up until that point.

Now, I can't protect you from everything. If someone joins late, they're still
gonna show up here and you'll know they exist. Also, if someone does something
notable in a newer episode, it might be mentioned here even if you haven't
seen that far. Sorry about that.

Also. This might not be totally up to date with the most recent episodes,
because as I said, I'm behind! If you find a bug or mistake, please let me know
at ifthencreate@gmail.com.
